# The DepthRangers RPG system by BIT-Mysteries

**DepthRangers** is an Open Source rules light RPG System. It's leaner than than something like *Basic Fantasy* but more complex than something like *Four Against Darkness*. The goal is to get you on your feet quickly or rather deep into underground trouble.

**DepthRangers**'s focus is on fast combat-heavy gameplay; yet is flexible enough to provide varied experiences, as well as light RPG flavoring.

## Who created this?

This System is a [BIT Mysteries Product](https://bajainnotech.github.io/bit-mysteries/), created by Eduardo del Corral, Ehira Lira & Hyunjin Oh.

Artwork was created by Ehira A. Lira with digital image editing done by Eduardo del Corral.

### Origin of the System

In the process of deleoping a solo campaign (soon to be released), we fused our influences and ended up creating our own RPG System which we call **DepthRangers**. It was designed taking inspiration from [*Crawlers! Micro RPG* System by *Austin Rowlett*](https://dr-sleepysloth.itch.io/crawlers-a-micro-rpg), and [*WOTC's SRD 5.1*](https://dnd.wizards.com/resources/systems-reference-document) but has taken a life of its own.
